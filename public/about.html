About Us & Business Overview

Beast Arena is an entertainment-driven esports organizer creating high-stakes, challenge-based tournament videos inspired by the global creator format. We bridge the gap between casual gamers and massive audiences by hosting curated matches in Minecraft, Valorant, BGMI, and more. Our business model focuses on zero-cost player entries, completely brand-sponsored prize pools, and digital content distribution via YouTube.
