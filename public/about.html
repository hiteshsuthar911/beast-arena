About Beast Arena

Beast Arena is an entertainment-driven esports organizer creating high-stakes, challenge-based tournaments inspired by the global creator format. We bridge the gap between casual gamers and massive audiences by hosting curated matches in Minecraft, Valorant, BGMI, and more.

Our model is built on three pillars: zero-cost entry, brand-sponsored prize pools, and high-quality video content distributed across YouTube and social platforms.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratise competitive gaming in India — giving every skilled player a fair, free, and professionally produced platform to compete, grow, and get recognised.
