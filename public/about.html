About Beast Arena

Beast Arena is an entertainment-driven esports organizer creating high-stakes, challenge-based tournament videos inspired by the global creator format. We bridge the gap between casual gamers and massive audiences by hosting curated competitive matches in Minecraft, Valorant, BGMI, and more.

Our business model is built on three pillars: zero-cost player entries, completely brand-sponsored prize pools, and high-quality digital content distributed across YouTube and social platforms. Every tournament is a production — not just a competition.

🎯 Our Mission

To democratise competitive gaming in India by giving every skilled player — regardless of background — a fair, free, and professionally produced platform to compete, grow, and get recognised.

🏅 Core Values

🆓
100% Free Entry
No registration fees — ever. All shortlisted players compete at zero cost. Skill is the only barrier to entry.
💸
Transparent Payouts
Prize pools are fully brand-sponsored and paid out transparently. Winners receive funds the same day results are announced.
🎬
Premium Production
Every tournament is filmed, edited, and published as high-quality content. Players become part of something bigger than just a match.
🛡️
Zero Toxicity Policy
Cheating, hacking, or toxic behaviour results in immediate and permanent disqualification. We protect our competitive integrity above all.

Hitesh brings together his knowledge of gaming culture, content production, and brand partnerships to run Beast Arena as a fully sustainable, player-first esports operation. His vision is to build India's most recognised entertainment esports brand — one tournament at a time.
